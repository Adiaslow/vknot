About

I'm a computational cyclic peptide drug discovery researcher specializing in geometric deep learning for molecular design and property prediction.

This site is a research notebook where I share work that I find novel, scientifically valuable, mathematically beautiful, or just plain fun. Posts range from rigorous theoretical frameworks to playful explorations, and include both complete work and ideas with theoretical foundations awaiting implementation.

Research interests include:

  • Geometric deep learning
  • Graph theory and network analysis
  • Stochastic optimization
  • Genetic algorithms and evolutionary computation
  • Dynamic systems and chaos theory
  • Multi-agent systems
  • Information theory
  • Differential geometry
  • Game theory and competitive dynamics
  • Complex systems

The posts here are representative examples of my work and interests, unified by an emphasis on mathematical rigor, computational methods, and the occasional delightful tangent into game theory or cellular automata.
