Soft Systems

Featured Artist
California • 1 release

Soft Systems explores the intersection of algorithmic composition, field recording, and ambient electronics. Working with processed environmental sound and generative synthesis, the project creates immersive soundscapes that examine themes of impermanence, fragility, and the beauty found in transitional states.

Electronic Ambient Experimental Electroacoustic

Approach & Methods

Generative and algorithmic composition techniques
Field recording and environmental sound processing
Granular and spectral synthesis
Real-time audio manipulation and live coding
