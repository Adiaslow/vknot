Life Is Fragile

by Soft Systems

The debut from Soft Systems. Seven tracks in fifteen minutes, rooted in a life that began in the sharp hum of a NICU and has been softening toward the world ever since. Everything arrives at once — warm tones that fill the room and stay, sudden bright ruptures that offer no warning and no explanation. Between the jaggedness, gentleness. Between the overwhelm, grace. The album sits inside the too-muchness of things and settles where it can, finding love and peace in the spaces a lifelong sharpness leaves behind.
